Sam H. | Helpful Digital Design

Hey, I'm Sam, a UX/UI designer based in Brisbane, Australia. I used to write film and music reviews for a local arts mag, I'm insanely fond of the weird fiction of Jeff VanderMeer, used to play the Bassoon, and prefer my coffee via a twice daily ritual of obsessively prepared 6 cup moka pot. As an interface designer, I have a healthy fatigue of all things digital. This acts as a bit of a lighthouse, I feel, in steering large ships (as best I can) away from the rocky shores of ~delight over all else~ and ~optimised hyper-personalisation~. I'm definitely still learning, I definitely don't know it all, and I definitely want to use my meagre powers for good. fAs a designer, my strengths are in wireframing and prototyping, user-testing,.
